About Us

Our Mission

Building the next generation of tech leaders through accessible, engaging, and comprehensive coding education.

Who We Are

Coding4Kidz is a non-profit organization created at Del Norte High School with the mission to teach kids basic coding and introduce them to the STEM field. Our sessions typically last about 2 months and cover Scratch, Python, and Java programming.

Passionate Teaching

Our instructors are dedicated students who genuinely care about sharing their love for coding

Small Classes

Personalized attention with low student-to-teacher ratios

Hands-on Projects

Build real projects that showcase your newfound skills

"Our mission is to democratize programming education, making it accessible, engaging, and inspiring for young learners everywhere. We believe that coding is not just about writing code‚Äîit's about problem-solving, creativity, and building the tools to shape our digital future."
